About Stelton Consulting

Redefining Talent Acquisition

At Stelton Consulting, we merge human expertise with artificial intelligence to redefine how companies discover and hire talent. Our intelligent recruitment engine uses AI-powered sourcing, predictive matching, and automated engagement, while our expert recruiters bring empathy, communication, and strategic insight — creating a partnership that delivers precision and performance.

Unlike traditional staffing firms, we go beyond keyword-based matching. Our proprietary candidate intelligence platform continuously learns from every placement, improving quality, diversity, and retention with every hire.
